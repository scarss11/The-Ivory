About The Ivory

Located in the heart of Barranquilla, The Ivory represents the pinnacle of Caribbean coastal cuisine, where traditional flavors meet contemporary sophistication.

Our commitment to excellence drives every aspect of our culinary journey, from the careful selection of the freshest ingredients from local markets to the artful presentation of each dish. We celebrate the rich gastronomic heritage of Barranquilla while embracing modern culinary techniques.

At The Ivory, we believe that dining is an experience that engages all the senses, creating memories that last a lifetime.
